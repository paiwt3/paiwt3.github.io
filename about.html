About

Take turns completing Unique Peelable Explosions on a Paiwt Board using ArtWaters materials. Paiwt is a creative art game for two players, played one vs one, who take turns completing Unique Peelable Explosions on a Paiwt Board using ArtWaters materials as directed by a deck of Paiwt Cards. The Paiwt Board consists of five three layered starting areas, one in the middle and four in the corners. The Paiwt Cards indicate which Pour, Drop, Brush, and Blow can be used during a player's turn. Each player starts with tools to create their Unique Peelable Explosions: seven different dropper bottles of ArtWaters Colors (red, green, blue, yellow, orange, pink, purple), seven different squeeze bottles of ArtWaters Colored Mediums (also red, green, blue, yellow, orange, pink, and purple), a paint brush, and a straw. The Paiwt Cards full deck consists of 42 cards: (14) two of each seven ArtWaters Color Drop (14) two of each seven ArtWaters Colored Medium (7) three Blow, two Blow + Drop*, two Pour + Blow* (7) three Brush, two Brush + Drop*, two Pour + Brush* *Two card combinations must be combined with their card, i.e. a Pour + Brush must be used with an ArtWaters Medium Paiwt Card, and a Blow + Drop must be used with an ArtWaters Color Drop Paiwt Card.
