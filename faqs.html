FAQs

What is Paiwt?

Paiwt is an art creation game using the Crosswaters™️ method to ultimately generate cryptocurrency. Players create Unique Paiwt Explosions according to the game rules and then submit scans of their work for cryptocurrency rewards and bonuses.

What does a Paiwt game contain?

Everything you need to play comes with Paiwt - The Art Creation Game: Paiwt Board, Paiwt Cards, Crosswaters™ Colored Medium Set, Crosswaters™ Colors Set, Paint Brush, Straw, Five Ernie Paiwt-Hands, Instructions

What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency (or crypto currency) is a digital asset designed to work as a medium of exchange that uses cryptography to secure its transactions, to control the creation of additional units, and to verify the transfer of assets.

What do I need to acquire cryptocurrency from Paiwt?

Paiwt - The Art Creation Game, Computer or Smartphone, Scanner

What is an Ernie Paiwt-Hand?

An Ernie Paiwt -Hand is a game piece that contains cryptocurrency information and a unique code used for block entry and subsequent randomization into the bonuses schedule.

What is a Cryptocollectable?

The real-world analogy for a cryptocurrency is dollars; the real world analogy for a cryptocollectable is closer to assets like stamps, silver/gold coins, comic-books, trading cards, or fine art.

What is a Unique Paiwt Explosion™?

A Unique Paiwt Explosion™ (UPE) is the final scannable art creation that is peeled off of the game board surface after the game is over. Its approximate diameter is about 3 inches. Explosions are unique because Crosswaters ™ media, colors, and methodology are used.

What are Crosswaters™ media?

Crosswaters™ media is a specially formulated water soluble non toxic mixture consisting of a binder mixed with water, chalk, gypsum, and colored dye. Paiwt contains seven different colors of Crosswaters™ media.

What are Crosswaters™ colors ?

Crosswaters™ colors are specially formulated water soluble non toxic dyes. They are essential, along with Crosswaters™ media, to produce the unique fractal effect. Paiwt contains seven different Crosswaters™ colors.

What are the scan requirements or specifications for submitting?

Scans must adhere to the following: original not edited 8” x 10” full scan , *.jpeg format, atleast 300k

How is every UPE unique?

The Crosswaters™ method️ creates unique fractal designs with infinite possibilities. The Paiwt rules add another layer of complexity that further guarantees irreproducibility.

Why do the Unique Paiwt Explosions peel off?

Crosswaters™ media only adheres to porous surfaces, and because it drys to form a flexible film, when they are applied to nonporous surfaces they can be peeled off and remain intact. The resulting skins can be used in a variety of ways including conversion to cryptocurrency.

Why are Paiwt cryptocollectables valuable?

Paiwt uses block technology to store various cryptocurrencys on handmade unique art creations known as Unique Paiwt Explosions. Paiwt is an extension of the Crosswaters™ method, the only water based methodology in the world that produces such an intricately beautiful effect. Crosswaters™ creations by themselves are valuable. Every beautiful organic fractal effect is unique. No two could ever be the same.Paiwt provides players of most ages and abilities the tools to create beautiful cryptocollectables which can be subsequently converted into cryptocurrency.

How are UPE prices calculated?

Each approved UPE starts with 2(USD) of your Ernie-Paiwt Hands cryptocurrency, and start with a standard selling price of 5(USD) of that cryptocurrency.

How are UPE sold?

Buyers take part in a silent auction by bidding on pieces once a month starting at 5(USD) of the Unique Paiwt Explosions assigned cryptocurrency.
