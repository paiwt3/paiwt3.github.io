About

Paiwt [PAWNT] is a board game where your creations create cryptocurrency. Players create Unique Paiwt Explosions according to the game rules and then submit scans of their work for cryptocurrency rewards and bonuses. Individual Unique Paiwt Explosions will have a distinct visual appearance determined by the sequence of colors players choose during the game. After Unique Paiwt Explosions become part of a block, they can be bought, sold, or transferred digitally. All the block digital Unique Paiwt Explosions are unique and 100% owned and controlled by the artists; they cannot be replicated, stolen, or destroyed. Unique Paiwt Explosions are not a cryptocurrency; they’re a cryptocollectible.
